## Standard Job Unlocking

### Ability Requirements

Most basic jobs are unlocked by learning a certain number of abilities from prerequisite jobs. To check requirements:

1. **Navigate** to the job selection screen
2. **Hover** over the locked job
3. **Press Y** to view requirements

### Common Prerequisites

- **Basic Jobs**: Usually require 2-4 abilities from starter jobs
- **Advanced Jobs**: May require abilities from multiple job branches
- **Master Jobs**: Often require mastery of several related jobs

## Quick Reference

### High Priority Quest Jobs

These jobs are essential for optimal builds and should be unlocked early:

**For Physical Builds:**

- Fighter (basic physical abilities)
- Parivir (highest WATK growth for Humes)
- Dragoon (excellent Bangaa physical job)

**For Magical Builds:**

- Seer (highest MDEF growth for Humes)
- Arcanist (high MP, good for Nu Mou)
- Geomancer (defensive magic job for Gria)

**For Support/Utility:**

- Beastmaster (for Blue Mage monster skill learning)
- Hunter (balanced stats, ranged abilities)
- Assassin (highest SPD for Viera)

### Story Progression

Some quest jobs become available naturally through story progression, while others require seeking out specific NPCs or meeting certain conditions. Consult a full quest guide for detailed unlock conditions.
